Honolulu Flag Redesign

I chose to feature a yellow lei on my flag, comprised of Oahu's official flowers, the ‘ilima. There are a total of 8 flowers to symbolize the 8 islands, with a slight emphasis on the top flower to express that while all islands are equal, the flag is meant to represent a single island. Golden Yellow (#FFC000) is the color of Oahu along with the traditional green of UH Manoa (#024731).

"Good" Flag, "Bad" Flag

According to the North American Vexillological Association, there are five key principles that distinguish “good” flag design from “Bad” flag design. I let these five principles guide my design resulting in a flag that is both simple and meaningful.

Current Honolulu Flag Design

The Five Principles:

  1. Keep It Simple.
  2. Use Meaningful Symbolism.
  3. Use 2 or 3 Basic Colors.
  4. No Lettering or Seals.
  5. Be Distinctive or Be Related.
