Honolulu Flag Redesign

I chose to feature a yellow lei on my flag, comprised of Oahu's official flowers, the ‘ilima. There are a total of 8 flowers to symbolize the 8 islands, with a slight emphasis on the top flower to express that while all islands are equal, the flag is meant to represent a single island. Golden Yellow (#FFC000) is the color of Oahu along with the traditional green of UH Manoa (#024731).

"Good" Flag, "Bad" Flag

According to the North American Vexillological Association, there are five key principles that distinguish “good” flag design from “Bad” flag design. I let these five principles guide my design resulting in a flag that is both simple and meaningful.

Current Honolulu Flag Design

The Five Principles:

  1. Keep It Simple.
  2. Use Meaningful Symbolism.
  3. Use 2 or 3 Basic Colors.
  4. No Lettering or Seals.
  5. Be Distinctive or Be Related.

La Perla Logo Redesign

La Perla is an Italian luxury lifestyle company founded by corset-maker Ada Masotti in Bologna Italy, 1954. Bologna is an Italian town renowned for its tradition of silk factories and textile manufacturers. Their current logo is typographic in nature, rather than symbolic.

Design Process

Conceptual Rationalizations

Pearl in the Oyster

Superior Title Light

This concept was designed with the word "classic" in mind. The shell of the oyster was meant to embody the structure of a corset, honoring Ada Masotti’s background as a corsetière. The brand name reflected the elegant way in which she would package her products, the reveal being compared to finding a pearl in the oyster - a precious jewel. This design was paired with Superior Title Light, a serif typeface that is both simple and elegant.

Italian Grey Wolf

DomLovesMary Pro

The Italian Wolf is the official animal of Italy. They are beautiful and fierce like the women who wear La Perla silk. Great care was taken to design the frame surrounding the wolf, utilizing the shape of the mulberry leaf, the silkworm’s delicacy. The wolf itself was designed with the words “feminine” and “confident” in mind. This design was paired with the scripted display font, DomLovesMary Pro.

Lace Moth

Felix Titling

When it is time to mature, silkworms surround themselves in silk cocoons and emerge as beautiful moths. Similarly, adorning oneself in La Perla silk brings out the beauty from within. The body of the moth was inspired by the bust of a traditional corset. The distinction of it being a moth and not a butterfly is important; the easiest way to make that distinction is by looking at the antennae. A butterfly has stalk-like, ball-tipped feelers whereas a moth has a thicker, almost feathery antennae which I emphasize in my design.
